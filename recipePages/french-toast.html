French Toast

Description :

Lovely french delicesant protein and fat and carbs in one, albeit low on the protein, and high in calories, but who cares when its this damn juicy

Ingredients :

  • Bread
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Berries
  • maple Syrup

Steps :

  1. whisk together egg and some cinnamon and optionally vanilla extract, whisk alot and fast to add a lot of air into the mix

  2. Dunk bread slices (brioche is best) in mix

  3. fry and cook on a frypan with butter

  4. add some maple syrup and butter and berries on top

  5. Voila French Toast!
