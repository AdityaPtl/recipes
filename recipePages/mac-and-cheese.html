Mac & Cheese

Description :

add some fried chicken, veg, some fresh bread rolls, and your sauce of choice (ranch) and you got meal to write home about

Ingredients :

  • Macaroni
  • Milk
  • Cheese (cheddar and moz)
  • Butter
  • Light spices
  • Flour

Steps :

  1. Cook macaroni (add in some butter)

  2. Make the roux, in saucepan, melt butter on medium, add flour and stir

  3. add milk and seasonings then wait for the sauce to thicken while constantly stirring its done when it coasts the back of a wooden spoon, or you can draw a path like moses through it.

  4. add cheese and fix the sauce to taste, add in cooked macaroni

  5. optinally add some breadcrubs and bacon bits on top and bake

  6. Voila Mac & Cheese!
