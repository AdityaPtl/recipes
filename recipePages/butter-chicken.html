Butter Chicken

  1. Marinate chubed chicken pieces (breast or thigh) overnight in indian yoghurt, cumin, turmeric, chilli powder, salt, pepper, and garam masala

  2. Add oil to frypan (a smaller sized wok works really well) and start roasting whole spices, after aromatic add roughly chopped onions and then add garlic and ginger paste and chillies

  3. start cooking chicken at the same time, now add in jarred tomato sauce to the sauce base (paste+ water works well too) give it a few minutes and add heavy cream and some butter

  4. blitz the sauce, add in some more butter and spices and all that according to taste and now add in cooked chicken and let it all mix together

  5. Voila Butter Chicken!
