Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description :

Nothing better for an easy lunch, 2 min prep, sugar, fat, protein, carbs, sugar, quick easy and portable, certified classic

Ingredients :

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter (crunchy)
  • Strawberry Jam

Steps :

  1. Spread jam on one side

  2. Spread peanut butter on other side

  3. Slam together both sides

  4. Pay the peanut butter tax to your dog

  5. Voila PB & J!
